Present your products in userfriendly and easy way with the Splendid Product Viewer.

== Description ==

The Splendid Product Viewer is a very easy to install and to maintain plugin. In a few steps you have your products inserted and the option to let your clients filter your products by color, price and product group. To each product you can add the thumbnail and the corresponding Main Image directly in your post.

== Installation ==

= Installation =

1. Upload the `product-viewer` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the WordPress Splendid Product Viewer plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress
1. You are ready to start!

= Add your products = 

1. Choose your Main Product Image
1. Enter your Price (not compulsory)
1. Select your color using the colorpicker (not compulsory)
1. Choose the Product Thumbnail Image
1. Choose or add the Product Category
1. Save the page

= Make the Splendid Product Viewer visible =

1. Choose the page, where you wish to add the Product Viewer
1. Paste the following code on the selected page: `[splendid-product-viewer]`
1. Update the page
1. The Splendid Product Viewer should be visible now
